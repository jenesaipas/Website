Susan was originally drawn to photography and "visually communicating", so she went to college for Digital Media Arts in Hawaii where she learned about everything from photoshop, digital and b&w photography, video, web to textile design. At this point, she mostly leaned towards photography and graphic design.

After wrapping up college, she moved to Seattle to pursue all the excitement and to see what she liked the best. It wasn't until 2012 when she decided to update her own website when she realized that the web is an even more intriguing technical and creative outlet to engage web users. In 2013 she started taking classes for Web Development to get an Associates Degree. She graduated in 2015 and currently is a downtown dweller in Minneapolis and keeps expanding her skillset. Right now her best skills and practices are UI/Front-End Development overlapping with web design.
